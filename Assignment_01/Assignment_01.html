RENDEZVOUS WITH NATURE

Built into a stunning hillside, overlooking panoramic vistas of well-manicured tea gardens and boasting a unique architectural layout seemingly blending into the lush green ecosystem, Pranavam is an idyllic retreat for a refreshingly rejuvenating rendezvous with Mother Nature at her pristine best! Cocooned by the majestic Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve exemplifying the tropical forest biome within the Western Ghats and set amidst 150 acres of sustainable plantations of coffee, spices, Ayurvedic flora and evergreen rainforests, our mission is to offer you an unforgettable yet eco-friendly holiday with state-of-the-art creature comforts for you and your family, far from the madding crowds and concrete jungles of urban living. From the moment you arrive, it feels like you've been transported to a magical wonderland that is captivatingly refreshing. Epitomizing quietude and tranquility, the accommodations offer absolute privacy with chalet-style cottages, a multi-cuisine restaurant, homemade authentic fare, an infinity swimming pool, and a spa and wellness center – all complemented with discreetly impeccable service. Located at Vythiri in Wayanad, Pranavam is easily accessible by road in just a couple of hours from all major cities across the South Indian states of Kerala, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. Whether you're looking for a laidback holiday or a fun-filled and adventurous vacation, we offer a host of facilities and things-to-do that are surprisingly varied.

Premium Cottages

These are spic-and-span, elegantly furnished, generously roomy and plush, offering 180-degree vistas of the stunning mountain ranges. The king-sized beds boast comfortable box spring mattresses. The en-suite bathrooms are large, hygienic, sparkling clean, and fitted with state-of-the-art mod-cons. The large deck overlooking the scenic views is perfect to unwind with a glass of wine. All cottages come fitted with free Wi-Fi, Cable TV and 24/7 room service.
