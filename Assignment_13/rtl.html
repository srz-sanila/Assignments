Image Gallery

Rose

With hundreds of species, roses bloom in a myriad of beautiful colors. Plant them where they'll get enough morning sun, and make sure to water them diligently.

Petunias

These trumpet-shaped flowers are popular to plant in flowerbeds. They come in a variety of colors, and they're easy to grow.

Gardenia

Known for its rich scent, this waxy flower with glossy thick leaves is often used in perfumes. An evergreen shrub that can grow 8 feet tall.

Alstroemerias

Typically called Peruvian lilies, they come in bright colors like pink, orange, and purple. Great for flower bouquets, they last up to two weeks once cut.
